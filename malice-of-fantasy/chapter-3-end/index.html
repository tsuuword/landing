Chapter 2 「Yuki and the Last Magic」

As the four of them were talking in the hospital room, the door opened. It was the adult in the white coat... …Honda Kazu. "Sumizora Mary has stolen documents from the hospital", he said. "There have been many suspicious activities, but actions were taken before evidence could be presented." "The Philosopher's Stone is undoubtedly the work of Sumizora Mary." He spoke dispassionately, without emotion. The four listened to his words absentmindedly. After delivering the message, the man in the white coat left the room. It was Doctor Malice who stole the Philosopher's Stone. The evidence found after searching the hospital and the fact that Doctor Malice, who had been present every day, suddenly disappeared were more than enough to believe those words. With Harley's Pegasus gone, Yuki unable to use magic, Taiyo and Hoshino's Philosopher's Stone stolen, and now even Doctor Malice went missing... That day, the four of them fell asleep without saying a word.

*Click* *Clack* *Click* *Click* *Clack* In the staff room, a man in a white coat illuminated by the monitor's light, typing away on the keyboard. Many screens displayed surveillance footage from various locations within the hospital. Subject number 028「Harley」 The fantasy of Pegasus disappears. Success in convincing Harley that Pegasus died of old age by preparing various materials in the library. July 6th. Safely freed from the fantasy. Subject number 055「Yuki」 Harbouring the fantasy of being able to use magic. Failed to persuade her to never use it again. Changed to Plan B. Successfully convinced her that magic users lose their ability to use magic at the age of 10. July 6th. Safely freed from the illusion. Subject number 099「Hoshino」 and Subject number 100「Taiyo」 Harbouring a shared fantasy. Fabricated a fictitious item called the Philosopher's Stone. Shared fantasies are prone to inconsistencies, and subjects harbouring shared fantasies have failed to break free from them in the past. Successfully convinced that the Philosopher's Stone was stolen by Sumizora Mary. July 7th. Safely freed from the illusion. As he finished typing, the man in the white coat exhaled deeply and slumped into his chair. ―――In the next moment, there was an explosion.
