「Hoshino」 Under the big tree in the courtyard, we buried a box with the Philosopher's Stone in it. It's a secret between Taiyo and me. However, about a week ago, I might have been seen by Yuki when I was digging alone to see the Philosopher's Stone. Yuki seemed unaware, but I'm worried if she found out about the stone. The Philosopher's Stone is a legendary stone said to grant any wish. Taiyo and I wanted to create the Philosopher's Stone! So, we polished a stone that was in the courtyard to create it. To be precise, it was created by chance. We don't feel like creating the same thing anymore. Of course, not every wish comes true…... but it shines beautifully in the dark. Taiyo said to me, "Hoshino, look! It's shining like the Philosopher's Stone in the picture book!", when I looked at the stone, it was indeed shining beautifully, so we decided to bury it in the courtyard as our own treasure. To prevent it from being easily dug up, Taiyo used the shovel he had to bury it a little deeper. "Someone stole the Philosopher's Stone!!", Taiyo is making a fuss in the hospital ward. The Philosopher's Stone was indeed missing from the box Taiyo brought. It was definitely there two nights ago. Who, and why would they do such a thing? The only ones who should know the hiding place of the Philosopher's Stone are Taiyo and me... No, maybe Yuki knows as well. Taiyo and I, our own treasure, stolen... I absolutely cannot forgive the person who stole it!!! (Goals) Find the person who stole the Philosopher's Stone.
