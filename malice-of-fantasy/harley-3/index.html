「Harley」 "Someone stole the Philosopher's Stone!!", Taiyo is causing a commotion in the hospital ward. The contents of the box Taiyo brought were empty. The box is covered in dirt. Did someone bury it somewhere? Come to think of it, three days ago, I felt like Taiyo was digging something in the courtyard. I don't really understand what the Philosopher's Stone is, but Taiyo seems filled with anger, and Hoshino also seems terribly upset. As Taiyo is saying, if someone stole it, there must be a reason. After spending time together like a family, I don't want it to end like this. For that reason, I need to listen carefully to Taiyo's story and find the thief. Yesterday, I misunderstood and got angry at Yuki, but with everyone's cooperation, we were able to resolve it. Find the person who stole it and make them apologise properly…... It will surely be okay. (Goals) Find the person who stole the Philosopher's Stone.
