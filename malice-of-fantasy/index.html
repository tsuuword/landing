Murder Mystery「Malice of Fantasy」

Player Count: 4, GM (Facilitator) not needed Duration: Approx. 2 hours 30 minutes This is a murder mystery for first-timers and beginners. As long as you have a communication application, such as Line or Discord, you can also play this with only a phone. When playing without a GM, one of the players has to be the facilitator. Once all players have opened this page, it is recommended to read through「Before playing murder mystery」to ensure a smooth gameplay.

White walls and ceilings formed the long corridors. Adults dressed in white coats with their cold stares, through the transparent glasses In the sterile room lies four beds. …...Doctor Malice. This world was everything for the children. 【Malice of Fantasy】

（Character Introduction） 「Harley」 11 years old, male; Eldest in the hospital. Characterised by his curly black hair. 「Yuki」 10 years old, female. Smart, always gets the highest scores on tests. 「Hoshino」 9 years old, female. The youngest but polite and mature. Skilled with her hands. 「Taiyo」 9 years old, male. Energetic boy, skilled with his hands and enjoys making things. Please discuss and decide which character each of you will be playing as. Once decided, please proceed to the next step.
