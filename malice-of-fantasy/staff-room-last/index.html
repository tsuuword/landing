Staff Room

【Honda Kazu's Body】 A man in a white coat is trapped under a shelf and appears to be dead. It's likely due to the earlier shock. 【Monitors】 All the monitors, which were numerous, have fallen and are completely destroyed due to the shock. 【Document】 Amidst the wreckage, papers labelled "Subject Records" have fallen. Subject Number 010 「Yu」 harbouring the fantasy of a phoenix, is deceased. 【Documents】 Among the scattered documents, another paper labelled "Subject Records" is found. Subject Number 011「Miyabi」who held the fantasy of magic, has also died. 【Door】 There's a door at the back of the staff room. However, it's locked and cannot be opened.
