「Taiyo」 In the morning, I woke up earlier than usual. Remembering the silly dream I had yesterday, I slipped out of the hospital ward alone and headed to the courtyard. Underneath the large tree in the courtyard, I buried a box with the Philosopher's Stone in it. The philosopher's stone is a legendary stone that is said to grant any wish, but of course, it's fake. Hoshino and I talked about wanting to make a philosopher's stone! We polished the stone in the courtyard and made something that looked like it. To be precise, it was made by chance. Of course, it doesn't grant any wish... but it does shine beautifully in the dark. It's amazing that we were able to make something like this. "We'll make it our own treasure!", and we decided to bury it in the courtyard. Using the shovel I brought from the room, I dug under the big tree in the courtyard and found a box. When I opened the box... the philosopher's stone was gone. I'm sure it was there two nights ago, Hoshino and I confirmed it. Who and why would someone do this? The only ones who should know the hiding place of the philosopher's stone are me and Hoshino. …...No, come to think of it, three days ago, I think Harley saw me digging a hole in the courtyard to see the philosopher's stone. I thought I wasn't found out at that time, but... Anyway, it's certain that it's gone. I can never forgive anyone who steals someone's treasure! (Goals) Find the person who stole the Philosopher's Stone.
